## Why I made this website?

I think the internet has been dying since 2020 and after that is what I think at least people making websites with no love like some old websites even if it was fully under construction for most of the people personal sites. The internet had a little charm to it I am not going to lie even if it was an eye sore you knew that someone put in time and effort into making even a simple *Hello!* on the screen. But now it's all AI and black and white. I want to restore that personality of the internet being close to and making people feel like I am there in person like it was meant to be. 

Even if I wasn't born back in the early internet era I still like to think of it as a place people coming together and just talking about their things and doing anything they like and sharing with the world. That is what I want to bring back from this website and I hope more people realize this and try the same.

While I went on a lot about that this is also my tech portfolio of things I am doing and making while I am studying.
